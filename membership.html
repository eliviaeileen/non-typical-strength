Membership

At Non-Typical Strength, members get 24/7 access to our fully equipped facility. Your membership includes a personal door code so you can train anytime that works for you.


Learn more about our facility amenities.


Membership Pricing:

  • $45/month — unlimited access with your door code
  • $500/year — unlimited access with savings
  • $10/day — day pass for anyone visiting

As our way of honoring local heroes, we offer a discounted membership of $35/month for first responders, healthcare workers, firefighters, law enforcement, and military personnel with valid ID.
